FAQ

What tools did you use to make DFtP?

Don't Forget the Phone was created using the Godot Engine. The models were created using Blender. Music and SFX were found on sites such as Freesound under CC licenses. Art was done by the very talented {Artist Name} (so we're not sure what tools they used).

Can I distribute your games?

No. At no time can you distribute our software. Any unlicensed distribution of our software will receive a take-down request and DMCA claim immediately.

Can I make a mod for DFtP?

Yes, you are permitted to create a mod for DFtP under these guidelines:

1. Your creation must clearly be advertised as a derivation of Don't Forget the Phone. Do not claim the mod is its own game.
2. Your creation must be made free-of-charge. You are not permitted to charge money for your mod.
3. You may NOT distribute a copy of Don't Forget the Phone with your mod. Your mod must be an addon to a purchased copy of Don't Forget the Phone (see "Can I distribute your games?").

Sandhill Software reserves the right to issue take-down & DMCA requests for any derivative work that may, or may not, fall under these guidelines.

These are only general guidelines, don't be malicious and you will be ok :]

Can I download the project file?

No. The project file for Don't Forget the Phone will not be made public. You can however view the source code here. You are also permitted to decompile the game using your own methods. However you are NOT PERMITTED UNDER ANY CIRCUMSTANCE to distribute the game in any form (see "Can I distribute your games?").
